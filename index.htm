Kaelble Atlas Loaders

KAELBLE ATLAS Heavy Wheel Loaders combine compact dimensions with impressive productivity for outstanding value. Their strengths really come to the fore in applications including earth moving, road construction, recycling, wood processing, agriculture and certain areas of the alternative energy industry.

Machines produced with more than 50 years experience, KAELBLE ATLAS has the specialist knowledge to deliver high quality machines for the widest range of applications.
